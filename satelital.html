A distant solar system awakens as strange artifacts across its worlds push a human expedition to uncover who built them and why.

Each planet presents its own unique puzzles, from rising islands on an ocean world to shifting forms over fractal landscapes, quiet lunar structures, and crystalline formations built around simple shapes.

Explore these environments, uncover hidden connections, and witness robotic centinels as your journey grows ever more cosmic. Can humanity master technology born from the heart of a star?
