Satelital is a sci-fi puzzle adventure where you control a high-tech satellite designed to study the unexplored Lumen solar system. As an astronaut on a mission, you'll journey between planets, using advanced sensors to interact with artifacts left behind by an unknown form of life.

Satelital is under development. Screenshots showcase the current state of the project, not the final result.

Key Features

• Solve puzzles

• Orbit in space

• Travel to distant planets

• Find natural patterns

• Experience peaceful landscapes

About

Miguel Dorta

I'm the solo indie developer behind Satelital. To bring to life an idea for a unique sci-fi puzzle adventure, I began working on a custom game engine back in 2020 because I enjoy creating custom tools from scratch and putting a lot of care into the details of the environments I make.

My goal is to create spaces that feel welcoming and encourage players to explore and discover things at their own pace.
