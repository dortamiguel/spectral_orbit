Satelital is a rule-discovery game about solving alien puzzles through observation and experimentation.

Travel between six celestial bodies, each featuring a unique set of rules. Place symbols on a grid and study their interactions to learn how they work. Each puzzle introduces a new idea and expands on what you've learned before, without lengthy tutorials or explicit explanations.

Listen to a calm ambient soundscape as you explore the cosmos at your own pace.
